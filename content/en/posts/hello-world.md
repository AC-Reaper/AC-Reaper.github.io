It's been a while since I've written anything properly.

Today I put together this blog with the most boring possible stack: Hugo plus GitHub Pages.
The whole thing is just a Markdown file and a `git push` away — a few seconds later it's live.

## Why another blog?

Over the years my notes have piled up across Notes, Notion, Obsidian, and a dozen chat threads.
When I actually want to find something later, it all blurs together. I want to try keeping a slice
of it in the open — not so much for anyone in particular, more for future-me.

## What I'll write about

- **Tech** — bugs I hit, tools I rely on, tiny projects that wake me up at 3am
- **Life** — fragments of trips, books and films, what I've been listening to
- **Random** — occasional half-formed thoughts

I won't force a schedule. **Write when I have something to say, skip otherwise** — that's the
only rule I'm giving myself.

## Before we start

If you happen to be reading this, hi 👋. You can find me on GitHub.

Let's begin.
